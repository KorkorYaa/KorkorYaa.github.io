MORE ABOUT US ?


Touring the whole coutry and beyond, we place smiles on the faces of those who meet us. We leave marks on the hearts of people, giving them alot of reasons to have hope. Making others happy has always been our drive and motivation.


OUR TOUR



We received a dignifying award in the World Cultural Dance Competition in Korea.
This Competetion involved alot of dance groups across the globe. Groups from China,America,Thailand,Phillipines and alot of places were hosted in South Korea to show their culture through their dance. The Righteous Stars won the third place as we displayed the beautiful culture of Africa through our performance.
